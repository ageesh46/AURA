Welcome to FitGAN AI

Your personalized fitness transformation partner. Unlock your potential through the power of AI and science-based fitness. Our intelligent engine designs dynamic diet and workout plans uniquely tailored to you.

Understand Your Body Type

Each body type has a different metabolic response, and AURA makes sure your plan fits perfectly. We analyze whether you are an Ectomorph (naturally slim), Mesomorph (naturally athletic), or Endomorph (naturally broader) to optimize your nutrition and training for the best results.

The Science Behind AURA

We calculate your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) and create a diet that balances Proteins, Carbohydrates, and Fats. Our AI adjusts sets, reps, and rest times based on your experience level and chosen goal, ensuring you are always progressing.

Visual Progress Tracking

Every user gets a side-by-side dotted biometric image showing your current physique vs. your dream physique. This is generated dynamically by our AI, helping you see your transformation, not just read numbers.
